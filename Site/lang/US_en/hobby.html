History of the Piano

The piano, invented at the beginning of the 18th century by Bartolomeo Cristofori, has undergone numerous evolutions...

It had variations such as the pianoforte before becoming the modern piano.

Types of pianos

There are different types of pianos: the upright piano, the grand piano, the digital piano...

Each type of piano has its own characteristics and is suitable for different needs and spaces.
